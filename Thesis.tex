\abstract{
\addtocontents{toc}{\vspace{1em}}  % Add a gap in the Contents, for aesthetics
Figure of the earth remains one of the most crucial aspects of geodesy. Modern studies of the figure of the earth have started with Gauss \cite{gauss} who call it `geoid'. The geoid according to Gauss is the surface that approximates the sea level. Another approach of describing the figure of the earth was proposed by Molodnskey \cite{molodonskey}. His proposal was to treat the earth's figure as `a boundary value problem'. We're interested in the later approach as we believe that it's economically feasible, yet accurate enough.

}
